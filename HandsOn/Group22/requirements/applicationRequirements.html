Application Requirements for Smart City Dataset Analysis

Objective

The objective of the application is to analyze and visualize data on traffic accidents in the city of Madrid to improve road safety and assist in informed decision-making by authorities and citizens.

Key Requirements

  • Access to open data on traffic accidents from 2010 to the current date.
  • Ability to filter and search information by year, type of incident, and location.
  • Graphical visualization of data, including maps and accident statistics.
  • Functionality to download data in CSV and Excel formats for further analysis.

Mock-ups

The application will feature:

  • An interactive dashboard displaying accident statistics by year and type.
  • Maps visualizing the location of traffic accidents throughout the city.
