Traffic Accident Rate

Introduction

Dataset related to the Traffic Accident Rate in the City of Madrid Traffic Accident Dataset

Requirements

  • R1: Data are in one of the smart city domains
    • The dataset belongs to Madrid City's transportation domain, which is included in the Smart Cities vocabulary.
  • R2: Data are available as a CSV file
    • Yes, the dataset is available in CSV format.
  • R3: Data have an open license so they can be published
    • datos.madrid.es is an open portal for datasets, meaning it is open-licensed. Their license details can be found here.
  • R4: Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities
    • This dataset includes real locations, vehicle types, gender, injury types, and alcohol and drug positivity tests.
