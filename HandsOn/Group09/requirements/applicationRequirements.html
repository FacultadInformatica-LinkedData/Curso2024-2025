Application Requirements Specification: Concesiones y Autorizaciones Management Application

Introduction

The proposed application aims to visualize and analyze the concessions and authorizations granted across various sectors in the city during 2023. The application will allow users to explore the status and details of the authorizations, helping both city officials and citizens monitor the permissions and regulations affecting public spaces.

Functional Requirements

  • FR1: Interactive map showing the locations of concessions and authorizations across the city, categorized by "Distrito" and "Situación".
  • FR2: Ability to filter concessions by sector, such as "INSTALACIONES DE RESTAURACIÓN" or "OTRAS CONCESIONES Y AUTORIZACIONES", and type of authorization.
  • FR3: Detailed view of each concession, including the type of authorization, granting authority, and validity period.
  • FR4: Search functionality to find concessions based on "Denominación" or "Número de archivo".

Non-Functional Requirements

  • NFR1: The application must be intuitive and easy to use, with an accessible design for users of all ages.
  • NFR2: The application should handle large datasets efficiently, ensuring minimal loading times for maps and data visualizations.
  • NFR3: Data should be updated regularly to reflect real-time changes in concessions and authorizations.

User Interface Mock-ups

Main Screen - Interactive Map

The main screen will feature an interactive map of the city, showing markers for each concession. Users can click on a marker to view detailed information about the concession, such as its "Denominación" and "Número de archivo".

Filter and Search Options

Users will be able to filter the concession data based on sector, type of authorization, or geographical location. A search bar will allow users to directly locate specific concessions by "Número de archivo" or "Denominación".

Concession Details View

When a user selects a concession on the map, a detailed view will appear, showing the type of authorization, the entity that granted it, and the date of issuance and expiry.

Conclusion

The proposed application will provide a valuable tool for both city authorities and citizens to monitor and manage the concessions and authorizations granted within the city. By offering an easy-to-use interface and real-time data, the application will support transparency and efficient urban management.
