Ontology

Parking

Represents the parking.

hasId, hasName, hasSchedule

Address

The address of the parking lot, the district, neighborhood.

hasDistrict, hasNeighbourhood, hasStreetNumber, hasStreet

Vehicle

Information about the vehicle types and the number of parking slots.

hasVehicleType, hasNumberSlots
